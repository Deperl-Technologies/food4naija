Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Food4Nigeria.

You can place an order directly on our website by Browse our product categories, adding items to your cart, and proceeding to checkout. Our platform is designed for a seamless shopping experience.

We accept various secure payment methods including major credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard) and local payment gateways. Cash on delivery is also available for select areas.

Delivery times vary depending on your location and the specific items ordered. Typically, deliveries are completed within 24-48 hours within Lagos. You will receive an estimated delivery window during checkout.

We strive for your satisfaction. If you are not happy with your order, please refer to our detailed Return Policy page for information on how to process returns and refunds.
