Delivery Information

Everything you need to know about getting your fresh produce delivered.

Our Delivery Process

1

Order Placement

Browse our wide selection of fresh produce and staples. Add your desired items to your cart and proceed to checkout. Ensure your delivery address is accurate to avoid delays.

2

Order Confirmation & Preparation

Once your order is placed, you'll receive an email confirmation. Our team then carefully selects and packages your items to ensure freshness and quality upon arrival.

3

Dispatch & Tracking

Your order is dispatched for delivery. You will receive a notification with tracking details (where applicable) so you can monitor its journey to your doorstep.

4

Delivery

Our dedicated delivery personnel will bring your fresh foodstuffs right to your specified location, ensuring a smooth and convenient experience.

Important Delivery Notes

  • **Delivery Zones:** We currently deliver within Lagos State. Please check if your area is covered during checkout. We are rapidly expanding our reach!
  • **Delivery Times:** Deliveries are typically made between 9:00 AM and 5:00 PM on weekdays. You can select your preferred delivery slot during checkout based on availability.
  • **Delivery Fees:** Delivery fees vary based on your location and order size. The exact fee will be calculated and displayed before you confirm your order.
  • **Minimum Order:** A minimum order value may apply for delivery. This will be clearly stated at the checkout.
  • **Reschedules & Cancellations:** If you need to reschedule or cancel an order, please contact our Help Center as soon as possible.
  • **Receiving Your Order:** Please ensure someone is available to receive your order at the designated delivery time. For perishable goods, prompt collection is recommended.
  • **Missed Deliveries:** In case of a missed delivery, our team will attempt to contact you to arrange a redelivery. Additional charges may apply for redelivery attempts.
